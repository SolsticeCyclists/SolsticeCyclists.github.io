Painting Party Packing List

  • Sunscreen to apply under your paint. Ask Rob about his plaid sunburn from Solstice 2002.
  • Yogurt containers for mixing paint
  • Paint brushes – the inexpensive foam type work well
  • Stencils
  • Bike decorations
  • (For outside painting) a carpet square or chunk of cardboard and an old towel to stand on while being painted.
  • Hot tea and snacks

Parade Packing list

  • Water (more than you think) and snacks
  • Sunscreen and lip balm – protect those sensitive bits
  • Bike helmet
  • Fabric scrap to wrap your bike seat – keeps the paint off
  • Clothes to don once we reach GasWorks. Think loose and breezy. Paint can be very uncomfortable (especially for guys) when confined to warm, tight fitting clothes.
  • Camera
