The Rules of the Ride

Be respectful of your fellow cyclists. We’re a big group on narrow streets and accidents do happen! Don’t weave or do tricks unless you’re well away from other cyclists. Keep to the right side of the street to allow others to loop back.Be respectful of parade spectators. The Fremont Solstice Parade is a family friendly event. Folks expect the painted cyclists, but they may not want your junk in their kid’s face. Use good judgment when interacting with spectators.

Be respectful of other parade participants. Don’t ever ride among the ensembles.

Don’t be impaired. There’s some tricky riding involved; alcohol and other drugs really don’t mix with this.
